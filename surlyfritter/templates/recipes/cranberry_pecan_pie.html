Cranberry Pecan Pie

{% endblock %} {% block ingredients%}
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2/3 cup meleted butter
  • 2 cups corn syrup
  • 6 eggs
  • 3 to 4 cups toasted pecans
  • 3 to 4 cups cranberries
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
{% endblock %} {% block instructions %}
  • Cook sugar, salt, butter, and corn syrup on stove
  • Add cranberries, pecans, eggs, and vanilla
