Joy of Cooking Pie Dough

{% endblock %} {% block ingredients%}
  • 6.5 oz frozen butter
  • 2.5 cups white all-purpose flour, pref frozen
  • 2 cups ice water
{% endblock %} {% block instructions %}
  • Cut frozen butter with cleaver into ~1-inch cubes
  • Blend butter and flour together in cuisinart until consistency of cornmeal. Should still be cold
  • Put flour/butter mixutre into large bowl and
  • Add ice water a quarter cup at a time, mixing until it becomes a workable dough
