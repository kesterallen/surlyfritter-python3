Pumpkin Pie

{% endblock %} {% block ingredients %}

Ingredients

  • 15 oz can pumpkin
  • 12 oz can evaporated milk
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ginger
  • 1/4 tsp cloves
  • 2 eggs
  • Unbaked crust
{% endblock %} {% block instructions %}

Instructions

  • Mix dry ingredients
  • Beat eggs
  • Stir pumpkin and dry ingredients into eggs
  • Gradually stir in milk
  • Bake at 425 F for 15 minutes, then 350 F for 30-40 minutes
