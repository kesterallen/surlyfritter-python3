Broccoli Noodles

{% endblock %} {% block ingredients %}

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp butter
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 3 diced garlic cloves
  • pepper & salt
  • 2 Tbsp flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 4 oz cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup parm cheese
  • extra cheese
  • 1 lb pasta
  • 1 lb chopped broccoli chopped small
{% endblock %} {% block instructions %}

Instructions

  • Cook pasta, not quite to al dente
  • In a separate pot saute onions in butter, 5 min
  • Add broccoli, garlic, salt, and pepper: cook 5 more min until broccoli is tender
  • Add flour, cook for 1 min, add milk 1/2 cup at a time and stir until combined
  • When milk is combined, add the in cheese
  • Add noodles to sauce, add more pasta water if needed
