Green Chile

{% endblock %} {% block ingredients%}

Ingredients

  • 3 med onions
  • 6 cloves garlic
  • 1 6 lb can green chiles
  • 3 (2?) lbs pork
  • 4 bay leaves
  • cumin
  • oregeno
  • 2 Tbsp all purpose flour
  • 4 Tbsp water
  • salt and pepper to taste
{% endblock %} {% block instructions %}

Instructions

  • brown pork in oil
  • Add onions and cook until translucent
  • Add chopped green chiles
  • Add bay leaves
  • Add spices
  • Bring mixture to a boil, reduce heat and simmer about 1 hour, stirring occasionally, until green chiles are falling apart.
  • Mix flour and water, stir into stew to thicken
  • Simmer 15 minutes more to cook flour
