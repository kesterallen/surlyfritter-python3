Black Dot's Chocolate Aunt Cake

By Jess Blanch

{% endblock %} {% block ingredients%}

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup baking cocoa
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 2 Tablespoons oil
  • 2 cups boiling water
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
{% endblock %} {% block instructions %}

Instructions

  • Grease and flour pans
  • In a large bowl, combine and sift dry ingredients
  • Add oil and water
  • Add eggs and vanilla
  • Bake 350F for 25m (bundt or 2 layers)
