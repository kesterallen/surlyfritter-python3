Instant Pot Steel-Cut Oatmeal

{% endblock %} {% block ingredients %}
  • Prep Time 5 minutes
  • Cook Time 45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups steel-cut oats
  • 5 cups water
  • 5 min manual cook setting on instant pot
{% endblock %} {% block instructions %}

Instructions

  • Put oats and water in the Instant Pot
  • Set the lid to "sealing"
  • Press the Manual button, set to 5 minutes (getting up to pressure will take ~20min)
  • After is complete, allow the pressure to naturally release for ~20 minutes. Extra time is fine
  • Stir oats to mix in any excess water

To set the delay timer on Instant Pot:

  • Press "Manual" button
  • Adjust the cooking time with +/- buttons
  • Press "Delay Start" button
  • Set the delay cooking time with +/- buttons
