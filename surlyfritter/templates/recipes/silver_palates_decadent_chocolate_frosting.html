Silver Palate's Decadent Chocolate Frosting

By Jess Blanch

{% endblock %} {% block ingredients%}

Ingredients

  • 2 Tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 3/4 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 6 Tablespoons heavy cream
  • 1 1/4 cups confectioner's sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanillla
{% endblock %} {% block instructions %}

Instructions

  • Place all ingredients in a heavy sauce pan over low heat
  • Whisk until chocolate is melted and smooth
  • Cool slightly, and check for consistency. Add more sugar if it's too thin.
  • Drizzle on cake
